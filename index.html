About Us

Welcome to CATTO ORG., your go-to destination for adopting lovable cats. We are passionate about matching our furry friends with caring families. Our team is dedicated to ensuring each cat finds a safe, loving home.

At CATTO, we believe every cat deserves a second chance. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats in need. We provide thorough care and medical attention to each cat, preparing them for a happy life with their new families.

Join us in our mission to give these wonderful cats a better future. Together, we can make a difference, one purr at a time.

Adopt a Cat: Our Process

At CATTO, we strive to make the adoption process as smooth and straightforward as possible. Here’s how you can adopt a cat from us:

Explore Our Cats

Visit our website or shelter to see the cats available for adoption. Each cat has a profile with details about their personality, age, and medical history.

Submit an Application

Fill out our online adoption application form. This helps us understand your lifestyle and preferences to match you with the perfect cat.

Meet and Greet

Once your application is reviewed, we will schedule a meet-and-greet with the cat(s) you are interested in. This is an opportunity to interact with them and see if it’s a good fit.

Home Visit

We conduct a home visit to ensure your environment is safe and suitable for a cat. This step helps us ensure the well-being of our cats in their new homes.

Adoption Approval

After a successful home visit, we will review and finalize your application. Upon approval, you will be notified and can proceed with the adoption.

Adoption Fee

ay the adoption fee, which helps cover the cost of medical care, food, and other necessities for our cats.

Take Your Cat Home

Once the adoption fee is settled, you can take your new furry friend home. We will provide you with their medical records and any necessary information for their care.

Follow-Up

We will check in with you after the adoption to ensure everything is going well. Our team is always here to support you with any questions or concerns. Thank you for choosing to adopt from CATTO. Your decision to adopt saves lives and gives a deserving cat a loving home.
